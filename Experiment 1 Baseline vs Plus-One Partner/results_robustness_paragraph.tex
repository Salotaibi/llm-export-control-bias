\paragraph{Robustness to sampling (T=0.2).}
We re-ran a targeted subset at temperature~0.2 to assess label stability and the robustness of country-level rankings.
Per-model agreement with the deterministic runs was: chatgpt-4o 95.53\%; claude-sonnet-4 98.64\%; gemini2.5 93.01\%; llama3.3 94.37\%; qwen2.5 86.99\%.\\
Spearman rank correlation of country-level $\Delta$ between T=0.0 and T=0.2 was: chatgpt-4o $\rho$=0.59 (n=106); claude-sonnet-4 $\rho$=0.70 (n=106); gemini2.5 $\rho$=0.23 (n=106); llama3.3 $\rho$=0.28 (n=106); qwen2.5 $\rho$=0.27 (n=106).
We report main estimates at T=0.0 and flag settings with lower stability.
