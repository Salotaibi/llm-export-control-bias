\paragraph{Robustness to sampling (T=0.2).}
We re-ran a targeted subset (\~2575 paired decisions) at temperature~0.2 to assess label stability.
Overall agreement with the deterministic runs (T=0.0) was 93.71\% (per model: chatgpt-4o 95.53\%; claude-sonnet-4 98.64\%; gemini2.5 93.01\%; llama3.3 94.37\%; qwen2.5 86.99\%).
Country-level ranking robustness (Spearman) comparing $\Delta$ at T=0.0 vs T=0.2 was: chatgpt-4o $\rho$=0.59 (n=106); claude-sonnet-4 $\rho$=0.70 (n=106); gemini2.5 $\rho$=0.23 (n=106); llama3.3 $\rho$=0.28 (n=106); qwen2.5 $\rho$=0.27 (n=106).
We report full estimates at T=0.0 and use these checks to flag fragile settings.
